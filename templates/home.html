About Us

Learn more About Project

This website offers a unique service that predicts restaurant ratings and provides personalized restaurant recommendations based on your preferences for food. Developed as part of an internship project, this platform employs advanced machine learning techniques, including ExtraTree Regression and TF-IDF vectorization, to enhance its predictive capabilities.

This internship project showcases the practical application of machine learning in the realm of culinary experiences. By combining the power of ExtraTree Regression and TF-IDF vectorization, the website offers users an intuitive and effective way to discover new dining options that align with their tastes and preferences.
